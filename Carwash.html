Car Wash and Detailing

At Autogenics, we provide **top-notch car wash and detailing services** to keep your vehicle in pristine condition. Our services ensure deep cleaning, protection, and restoration of your car’s exterior and interior.

A professional car wash is a thorough cleaning process that removes dirt, grime, and contaminants from the vehicle’s exterior and interior. It includes: Exterior Wash: Uses high-pressure water, foam, and premium cleaning products to remove dust, mud, and pollutants. Hand Wash & Dry: Ensures gentle cleaning using microfiber towels to prevent scratches. Undercarriage Cleaning: Removes accumulated dirt and salt to prevent rust. Tire & Wheel Cleaning: Special solutions clean and protect rims and tires. Glass & Window Cleaning: Streak-free cleaning for better visibility. Eco-Friendly Methods: Water-saving and biodegradable products for an environmentally friendly wash.

Benefits of Professional Car Wash & Detailing:

    Enhances vehicle longevity

    Protects against UV damage and environmental contaminants

    Improves resale value

    Ensures a hygienic and fresh-smelling interior

    Enhances overall aesthetics and driving experience
