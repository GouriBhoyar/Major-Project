Paint Protection Films

At Autogenics, we provide **top-notch Paint Protection Films** to keep your vehicle in pristine condition. Our services ensure deep cleaning, protection, and restoration of your car’s exterior and interior.

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a high-performance, transparent polyurethane film applied to a vehicle’s exterior to protect it from scratches, rock chips, road debris, and environmental contaminants. It is designed to preserve the original paint while maintaining the car’s glossy finish.

Benefits of Paint Protection Film:

    Shields against minor impacts from stones, road debris, and scratches.

    Prevents paint from fading and yellowing due to sun exposure.

    Heat-activated technology removes light scratches automatically.

    Water and dirt slide off, making cleaning easier

    Keeps the car in pristine condition, increasing its resale price.
